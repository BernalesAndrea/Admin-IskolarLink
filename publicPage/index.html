Iskolar ng Koronadal

About the Scholarship

The Iskolar ng Koronadal – Kabugwason Paglaum Program is a local government initiative that provides educational assistance to deserving students in Koronadal City.

Scholarship Board Members

  • Chairperson – Juan Dela Cruz
  • Vice Chairperson – Maria Santos
  • Member – Pedro Reyes

Scholarship Benefits

  • Monthly allowance
  • Tuition fee assistance
  • Book and school supplies support

Requirements

  • Completed Application Form
  • Certificate of Enrollment
  • Barangay Clearance
  • Latest Grade Report

How to Apply

Interested applicants must create an account, complete their profile, and submit the required documents through the online application system.

Application Requirements

Below is the definitive checklist of documents and steps applicants must prepare and complete to be considered for the Iskolar ng Koronadal — Kabugwason Paglaum scholarship. Follow the checklist carefully and upload all required documents when you create your scholar account.

Eligibility (basic)

  • Resident of Koronadal City
  • Current undergraduate student (or incoming first-year applicant) enrolled in a recognized institution
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Maintains the minimum GPA (as announced by the program)

Required Documents (primary)

1. Completed Application Form
Download the application form, fill it out completely, and sign where required.
Download Application Form (PDF)
2. Official Transcript of Records (TOR)
Latest and official copy from your school. If unavailable, submit a certified true copy or school-issued grade report.
3. Proof of Household Income
Examples: BIR Form 2316, Certificate of Indigency, latest pay slips of parent/guardian, or IFAP documents. Provide the most relevant documents for your household.
4. 2x2 ID Photos
At least two (2) recent 2x2 passport-style photos. Digital uploads should be clear and in JPG/PNG format.
5. Government-issued ID
Any valid ID such as PhilHealth, Passport, Driver's License, or National ID. Upload a clear photo or scan.
6. Copy of Senior High Certificate / Diploma (if applicable)
For incoming college freshmen, include SHS completion proof.
7. Letter of Recommendation
One (1) letter from a teacher, school official, or community leader who can attest to your character and need. Optional but recommended.
8. Statement of Purpose / Personal Essay
A short essay (300–500 words) describing why you need the scholarship and how it will help your studies and community.

How to Submit

  1. Create an account / Sign up at the top-right of the site.
  2. After verifying your account, log in to the scholar portal.
  3. Go to Submit Documents and upload each required file. Use the recommended file formats (PDF, JPG, PNG).
  4. Ensure filenames are descriptive (e.g., TOR_John_Doe.pdf).
  5. Review the uploaded documents and click Submit Application.

Accepted file formats: PDF, JPG, PNG. Max file size per upload: 10 MB (adjust server-side as needed).

Important Tips & Notes

  • Make sure each file is legible and not password-protected.
  • Provide honest and accurate information — false statements may disqualify the application.
  • If any required document is unavailable, attach an official explanation and contact the admin.
  • Deadlines: Check announcements for application windows and deadlines.

After Submission

Once you submit your application, the Scholarship Board will review documents. You will receive notifications via your account and the contact email you provided. Some applicants may be asked for clarifications or additional documents.
